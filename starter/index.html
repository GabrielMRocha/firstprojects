The Chicago Dice Game is played in 11 rounds, starting with throw 2, then going to throw 3 and continuing until throw 12.

In each throw each player takes his or her turn and rolls two dice, trying to roll the number of that throw.

For example, in the throw with number 2, you aim to roll a 1 on each dice giving you a total of 2.

Every player that rolls the number of the current throw gets a point and adds it to his overall score.

After all 11 throws are finished the round ends and the player with the higher number of points is declared the winner.
